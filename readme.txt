Free business loan calculator that lets entrepreneurs estimate monthly payments, calculate interest on loans, and understand the total cost of the business loan.

[https://www.calculator.io/business-loan-calculator/](https://www.calculator.io/business-loan-calculator/)

== Usage ==

Add the Business Loan Calculator shortcode to your page, post or sidebar:

`[ci_business_loan_calculator]`

Add the following code to your template where you would like the Business Loan Calculator to appear:

`<?php display_ci_business_loan_calculator(); ?>`

== Installation ==

1. Upload the Business Loan Calculator /ci_business_loan_calculator/ folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the Business Loan Calculator plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
